Screen Calibration Tool
Adjust the Virtual Light Table display to your personal screen:
  1. Grab some ruler or any other real world size indicator
  2. Select the distance you want to measure with the buttons above
  3. Hold the 0 cm/inch mark against the screen such that it corresponds with point A in the field above
  4. Find the mark on your ruler that fits to your size mode and click into the field
Below you will see your new calibration values. Once you are happy with your result, press "Calibrate". Zoom mode "1x" should now correspond with real world sizes.
1 cm ≙ 96 px
1 inch ≙ 243,84 px
